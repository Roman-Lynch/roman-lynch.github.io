Mustard Man

Mustard Man marks the debut feature film from Merlin Productions. Principal photography took place from June to August 2023, with a production budget of $7,000. After an extensive post-production process, which concluded in April 2024, the film had its official premiere at The Screening Room in Tucson, Arizona. Additionally, it was showcased at UCSB in California and CU Boulder in Colorado.

Zach Mund

Starring |

Zach Mund, Charlie Nevitt, Albert DiCesare, Natalie Sargent, Kyle Varra, Travis Griffin

Synopsis

College dimwit Ryan finds his roommate Bradley dead on the floor, with all signs pointing to him as the culprit. Believing him to have been murdered, Ryan recruits his best friend Marc to help investigate Bradley’s offbeat friend group at that night’s party. As the list of suspects dwindles, the pair must pretend as though Bradley is still alive, leading to an amusing bout of Weekend at Bernie’s style antics in classic whodunnit fashion.

Comos Institute Event

When |

September, 2024

Where |

University of Colorado Boulder

Production

Merlin Productions had the privilege of capturing a distinguished live event at the University of Colorado Boulder for the Cosmos Institute. The event featured a keynote address followed by an interactive Q&A session with Brendan McCord, the esteemed Founder and Chair of the Cosmos Institute. Through our expertise in event coverage and storytelling, Merlin Productions ensured the event's impactful moments were preserved to be shared with a wider audience.

Post-Production

Merlin Productions then used post-production resources to skillfully edit the live event footage into engaging, shareable content for social media platforms such as Instagram and Facebook. These concise, captivating posts were designed to maximize audience engagement and effectively promote the event across digital channels.
